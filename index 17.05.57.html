Spanish

Spanish is a Romance language spoken in Spain and in much of Central and South America (except Brazil) and several other countries. It is the second most widely spoken first language in the world, with more than 400 millon speakers. Would you like to learn it?

English

English is the most widely spoken language by total number of speakers, with more than one billion speakers. However, English is the third most widespread native language in the world, after Mandarin and Spanish. It is the most widely learned language and is the official language or one of the official languages in almost 60 sovereign states. Would you like to learn it?

French

French is the official language in states on the five continents and also the second most studied language in the world, behind English: 120 million students are learning French today. In addition to the 220 million native French speakers in the world, there are an estimated 72 million semi-speakers, a number that is constantly increasing. Would you like to learn it?
